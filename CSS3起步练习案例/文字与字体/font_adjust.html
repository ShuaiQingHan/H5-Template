font-size-adjust属性原先是CSS2所制定，但在CSS2.1时被删除，在CSS3中又被加入并更新定义。

font-family: Vani, "Times New Roman";font-size: 18px;font-size-adjust: 0.8;

Sometimes text will have to be clipped. For instance when it overflows the element’s box.
